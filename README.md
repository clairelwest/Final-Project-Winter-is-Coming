# Final-Project-Winter-is-Coming

# CSCI 1300 Final Project

## Theme

My final Project will be themed around Winterfell from Game of Thrones. The main character will be Sansa Stark, and the game will also feature Brienne of Tarth, Arya Stark, Jon Snow, and Petyr Baelish. Sansa is the Queen of the North, and as winter approaches will need to collect the necessary materials to care for her people.

## Goal

Sansa must complete three bundles before winter to keep her people safe and well fed. Sansa loses if she runs out of time before winter. Sansa will also lose if she runs out of respect, and in this scenario Petyr will arrive and take Winterfell. I may include a special win scenario which includes Arya killing Petyr which wins Sansa additional respect.
