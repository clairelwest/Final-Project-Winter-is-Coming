# CSCI Final Project

## Theme:
My final Project will be themed around Winterfell from Game of Thrones.
The main character will be Sansa Stark.

## Classes:
I will have a class for the player, the side characters, and for locations.

## Limited Resource:
My limited resource will be gold as well as respect. 
Sansa will have an initial treasury ballance and can gain or lose the respect of her people.

## Time:
Sansa will have a set number of days before winter comes, and days are spent by traveling or completing tasks.

## Extra Credit:

## Tradeoffs:
Petyr Baelish will me the "villan" of the game and Sansa can get gold from Petyr, but loses respect when she does this.

## Map:
The map will include the North in Westeros as well as the Vale.
Sansa lives in Winterfell, and has charatures and tasks there.
Sansa can travel to The Wall, a village just outside Winterfell, and The Vale.

## Win/ Loss Condition:
Sansa must complete three bundles before winter to keep her people safe and well fed.
Sansa loses if she runs out of time before winter.
Sansa will also lose if she runs out of respect, and Petyr will take Winterfell.
