# CSCI Final Project

## Theme:
My final Project will be themed around Winterfell from Game of Thrones. The main character will be Sansa Stark, and the game will also feature Brienne of Tarth, Arya Stark, Jon Snow, and Petyr Baelish. Sansa is the Queen of the North, and as winter approaches will need to collect the necessary materials to care for her people.

## Classes:
I will have a class for the player, the side characters, and for locations. These will contain functions for trust levels between characters, dialog, and more.

## Limited Resource:
My limited resource will be gold as well as respect. Sansa will have an initial treasury balance and can gain or lose the respect of her people. She will use gold to purchase the supplies she needs for the bundles, and she must not let her respect level drop too low.

## Time:
Sansa will have a set number of days before winter comes, and days are spent by traveling or completing tasks. Completing tasks around Winterfell will cost a portion of a day, and traveling will vary depending on the distance. She can talk to other characters and check the status of her preparations without a time cost.
 
## Extra Credit:
I want to include a smarter bundle planner that will tell the player what still needs to be collected, what is most important to collect, and where to collect it. This will be included in dialog with Brienne who is her friend and advisor at Winterfell and will prompt her with tasks and goals.

## Tradeoffs:
Petyr Baelish will be the "villain" of the game and will live in The Vale. Sansa can travel to The Vale to get gold from Petyr, but loses respect when she does this. Sansa will likely need to do this at least once.

## Map:
The map will include the North of Westeros as well as The Vale. Sansa lives in Winterfell, and has characters to interact with and tasks to complete there. Sansa can also travel to The Wall, a village just outside Winterfell, and The Vale. The map can be displayed at the player's request and will show where the player currently is.

## Win/ Loss Condition:
Sansa must complete three bundles before winter to keep her people safe and well fed. Sansa loses if she runs out of time before winter. Sansa will also lose if she runs out of respect, and in this scenario Petyr will arrive and take Winterfell. I may include a special win scenario which includes Arya killing Petyr which wins Sansa additional respect.
